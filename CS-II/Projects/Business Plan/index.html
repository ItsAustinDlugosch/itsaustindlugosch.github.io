ISP Proposal

TV360

Identity

TV360 manufactures easy to use and install TV and monitor mounts that are capable of rotating 360 degrees to beneift consumers seeking to utilize the potential of their screens.

Problem

Televisions and monitors are often not visible at certain angles, and TV360 allows for a more customizable space. Additionally, TX360 can be used for games and gaming features that require an open space, such as XBOX kinect and VR.

Solution

Our TV and monitor amounts can track people around the room and swivel to match their movement. Additionally, TV360 can be configured via remote control input to rotate on demand. If there is sufficient time, TV360 would like to expand into 3 dimentsional postioning in addition to rotation as well as being accomadating of wall mounts and stand mounts.

Target Market

The target audience is adults and college students who want to maximize the space in their homes and the capabilities of their TV screens and monitors.

Competition

TV mounts are sold worldwide by a variety of large international companies that focus on a variety of products. TV360 will specifically specialize in TV mounts. Our mount is unique in that it will combine many features while most competitors only include one of the features. It will allow for much more custimization of the position of screens compared to our competitors.

Revenue Streams

TV360 will sell primarily online and to local stores. Additionally, we can recieve orders through calls from infomericals.

Marketing Activities

TV360 will advertise through infomercials and targeted TikTok and Instagram advertisements, and TV360 will communicate directly with customers through social media. We will try and partner will specific VR companies to help market our product.

Expenses

First, the materials for the design including plastic, steel, cameras, and remote control transmitters. Second, the cost for advertisements. Finally, the cost of distribution.

Team and Key Roles

Currently, the only team member is the founder, Austin Dlugosch, but as the project expands, TV360 would like to add people that can assist in the five main areas of the project: optimizing the design, movement tracking, remote control, advertisement, and quality assurance.

Milestones

As the business grows, TV360 would like to expand its outreach further to distant markets via delivery.
