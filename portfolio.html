Digital Portfolio

A digital portfolio is an excellent way to publish your own accomplishments and feats in a clean and organized way. It's open for the public so anyone can view who you are and what it is that you do. They provide an opportunity to display what you have worked on and push you to keep improving and adding to what you have already done. It has pushed me to be creative and make sure everything is exactly as I want it on my page. It has been an invaluable experience to learn the basics of HTML and CSS.
